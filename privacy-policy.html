Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

PetPal ("we," "our," or "us") operates the PetPal mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • Location (city/neighborhood)
  • Apple ID information (if using Sign in with Apple)

Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent)
  • Log data (access times, pages viewed)

Content You Provide

  • Messages sent to other users
  • Booking requests and history
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Favorite dogs saved
  • Reports submitted about other users or content

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Connect dog seekers with dog owners
  • Process booking requests
  • Send notifications about bookings and messages
  • Improve our services
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud
  • Review and act on user reports within 24 hours

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With other users: Your profile (name, photo, ratings) is visible to facilitate connections
  • With service providers: Cloud hosting, analytics (with data protection agreements)
  • For legal reasons: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on secure cloud servers (Cloudflare)
  • We use encryption for data transmission
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • No method of transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security

Account Deletion

Your Right to Delete: You can delete your account at any time directly within the App.

To delete your account:

  • Open the App and go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Confirm your decision (you will be asked to type "DELETE" to confirm)

When you delete your account, we will permanently and immediately delete:

  • Your profile information (name, email, photo, bio)
  • All dogs you have listed
  • Your booking history
  • All messages and conversations
  • Your reviews (both given and received)
  • Your favorites list
  • Any reports you have submitted
  • Your block list

This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data (see above)
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Block other users
  • Report inappropriate content

To exercise these rights, contact us at: e0399104@u.nus.edu

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

Email: e0399104@u.nus.edu

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年1月28日

PetPal（"我们"）运营 PetPal 移动应用程序（"本应用"）。本隐私政策说明我们在您使用本应用时如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的信息。

我们收集的信息

个人信息

当您创建账户时，我们可能收集：

  • 姓名
  • 电子邮件地址
  • 头像照片
  • 位置（城市/社区）
  • Apple ID 信息（如果使用 Apple 登录）

使用信息

我们自动收集：

  • 设备信息（型号、操作系统）
  • 应用使用数据（使用的功能、花费时间）
  • 日志数据（访问时间、浏览页面）

您提供的内容

  • 发送给其他用户的消息
  • 预约请求和历史记录
  • 评价和评分
  • 收藏的狗狗
  • 提交的关于其他用户或内容的举报

我们如何使用您的信息

我们使用收集的信息来：

  • 提供和维护本应用
  • 连接寻狗者和狗主人
  • 处理预约请求
  • 发送有关预约和消息的通知
  • 改进我们的服务
  • 响应客户支持请求
  • 确保安全并防止欺诈
  • 在24小时内审核和处理用户举报

信息共享

我们不会出售您的个人信息。我们可能在以下情况分享信息：

  • 与其他用户：您的个人资料对其他用户可见，以促进连接
  • 与服务提供商：云托管、分析（有数据保护协议）
  • 出于法律原因：如法律要求或为保护权利和安全

账户删除

您的删除权利：您可以随时直接在应用内删除您的账户。

删除账户的步骤：

  • 打开应用，进入 个人资料 → 设置 → 删除账户
  • 确认您的决定（您需要输入"DELETE"来确认）

当您删除账户时，我们将立即永久删除：

  • 您的个人资料信息（姓名、邮箱、头像、简介）
  • 您发布的所有狗狗信息
  • 您的预约历史
  • 所有消息和对话
  • 您的评价（包括给出的和收到的）
  • 您的收藏列表
  • 您提交的任何举报
  • 您的屏蔽列表

此操作无法撤销。一旦删除，您的数据将无法恢复。

您的权利

您有权：

  • 访问您的个人数据
  • 更正不准确的数据
  • 删除您的账户和数据（见上文）
  • 导出您的数据
  • 选择退出营销通信
  • 屏蔽其他用户
  • 举报不当内容

如需行使这些权利，请联系我们：e0399104@u.nus.edu

儿童隐私

本应用不面向13岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集13岁以下儿童的信息。如果我们发现此类收集，我们将立即删除。
